Before you leave...


Text-Based Interaction


Type short 2-3 word commands and press the enter key.

Commands may include:

  • go north
  • go south
  • go east
  • go west
  • look
  • examine ___
  • open ___
  • pick up ___

    • Words that are bolded and capitalized may be interacted with and used in a command.

    Keyboard Navigation






    Use the up, right, down, and left arrow keys
    of the keyboard to move the character across the screen.

    Player Book


    This game is meant to be played using both the desktop and the book provided.

    Draw or write in the book when prompted by the game.

    There will be a set timer for you to complete the prompt in the book before continuing the game.

    After completing the game, you may either take the book with you, or leave it behind for others to enjoy.
